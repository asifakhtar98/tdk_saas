# TKD Tie Sheet Generator

## Product Brief

A free, standalone app for Taekwondo tournament organizers to instantly create, manage, and print professional tie sheets (brackets). Works offline on any tablet or phone — no account needed, no internet required. Built specifically for TKD with dojang separation and Red/Blue corner conventions.

**Who it's for:** Tournament directors, dojang owners, coaches, and event organizers running local, regional, or club-level Taekwondo competitions.

**The problem it solves:** Most organizers still draw brackets by hand or use clunky spreadsheets. This app lets you add players, tap Generate, and have a ready-to-print tie sheet in seconds.

---

## Features (v1.0)

### Bracket Formats
- ✅ Single Elimination — classic knockout bracket
- ✅ Double Elimination — winners and losers brackets with reset match
- ✅ Round Robin — everyone plays everyone, round-by-round table view
- ✅ 3rd Place (Bronze) Match — optional toggle for single elimination

### Player Management
- ✅ Quick-add players with first name, last name, and dojang/club
- ✅ CSV paste import — bulk-add players from a spreadsheet
- ✅ Drag-and-drop reorder seeding
- ✅ Remove individual players or clear all

### Smart Seeding
- ✅ Dojang Separation — teammates from the same school are automatically placed on opposite sides of the bracket so they don't face each other early
- ✅ BYE handling — odd numbers of players get automatic byes in round 1

### Live Scoring
- ✅ Tap any match to score it
- ✅ Choose winner by Red or Blue corner
- ✅ Result types: Points, Knockout, Disqualification, Withdrawal, Referee Decision
- ✅ Winner auto-advances to next match
- ✅ Loser drops to losers bracket (Double Elimination)
- ✅ Reset/undo any scored match with cascading rollback
- ✅ No-Show / Walkover support

### Print & Export
- ✅ PDF export — full bracket with player names, match results, and roster
- ✅ Landscape A4 layout
- ✅ Separate PDF templates for bracket formats and round robin

### Display
- ✅ Landscape-only orientation (locked)
- ✅ Interactive zoom and pan on bracket view
- ✅ Color-coded match status (pending / in-progress / completed)
- ✅ Player names on all match cards (not IDs)
- ✅ Round labels (Quarterfinals → Semifinals → Finals)
